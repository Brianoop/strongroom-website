WHAT IS STRONGROOM ?

StrongRoom is a mobile application built by Giant Pool Group LTD as a tool for clients to use when they need to hire a machine from the company for any kind of survey related work.

WHY WE BUILT STRONGROOM ?

FAST BOOKING

We are making the machine booking process faster than it used to be for all our clients

DOCUMENT STORAGE

We want our clients to be able to access and download their documents at any time without having to contact our offices first.

REALTIME UPDATES

We need our clients to be able to know the realtime availability statuses of our machines to avoid inconveniences caused due to misinformation about machine statuses.
